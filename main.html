⚖ Know Your Rights — Pune Cab Rules

→ Drivers cannot refuse a metered/app-booked fare. Refusal is punishable under Maharashtra motor vehicle rules.

→ If a driver demands more than the app fare, you can cancel without penalty — the cancellation charge doesn't apply if the driver violates fare terms.

→ File complaints at RTO Pune: 020-26128000 or via in-app grievance against inflated quoting.

→ Surge pricing must be disclosed before you confirm booking. Undisclosed post-booking surge = grounds for refund.

→ Screenshot the app fare before boarding — this is your evidence in disputes.
